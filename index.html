1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendWise ("we", "our", or "us"). SpendWise is a personal expense tracking application that helps you manage and monitor your daily expenses and income — without involving any real banking or financial transactions.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using SpendWise, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

⚠️ Important: SpendWise does not connect to your bank accounts, process real payments, or access any financial institution. All data is manually entered by you.
📦

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Info Email address, display name To create and manage your account
Expense Data Expense/income titles, amounts, dates, categories To provide the core tracking functionality
App Settings Currency preference, language, app lock preference To personalize your experience
Device Info Device type (for biometric/lock features only) To support app lock functionality

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, microphone access, or any other sensitive device information.

⚙️

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Create and maintain your SpendWise account
  • Store and display your expense and income records
  • Generate monthly summaries and budget reports
  • Send budget alert notifications (if enabled)
  • Restore your data when you log in on a new device
  • Respond to support requests

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

🔒

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore Database and Firebase Authentication), which complies with industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit and at rest.

  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth — we never store raw passwords
  • App Lock (biometric/PIN) data is stored locally on your device only
  • Your expense data is tied to your account and accessible only by you
🛡️ While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and enable App Lock for added protection.
🔗

5. Third-Party Services

SpendWise uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and cloud storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — App distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We recommend reviewing them.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or tracking tools.

✅

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data inside the app at any time
  • Edit: Update your name and email from your profile settings
  • Delete: Delete individual expense or income entries anytime
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
👨‍👩‍👧

7. Children's Privacy

SpendWise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Continued use of SpendWise after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
